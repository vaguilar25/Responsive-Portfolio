I was born in Guatemala city, I have 39 years old, mother of two teens. Currently live in SF, I really enjoy this beautiful city, it offers a lot of things to do, you just have to make time for everything :).

I like to travel, visit new places and also meet new people, on my free time I like to do exercise, go outdoors with my boys and I am passionate about photography. I am part of the clubs of photographers of Guatemala, which is a group of very talented photographers that meet once a month to show their work and participate in a contest to gain recognition in the club. I have gain a lot of recognitions because of my work and have exposed my work also in galleries.

I love dogs, I have two dogs, one is a pomeranian and the other one is a corgi, both are very funny guys and we are happy to have them as a part of our family.
